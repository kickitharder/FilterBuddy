FilterBuddy



ASCOM driver for the FilterBuddy controller for the SBIG CFW-9 filter wheel.

The FilterBuddy controller is connected to the SBIG CFW-9 9-pin female connector. The controller has an Arduino within it and it is connected to one of your PC's USB ports.

Your PC needs to have the appropriate driver for the Arduio. The official Arduino driver can be got by dowloading the Arduino IDE at the Arduino Webite

Once everything is connected, the FilterBuddy will be presented on a COM port. When selecting FilterBuddy in the ASCOM FilterWheel Chooser, click on the Properties button to select the COM port. Click on the "Check Connection" button to see if you are using the correct one.

Enjoy!
